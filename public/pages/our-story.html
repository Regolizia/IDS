OUR
AIM

Matty is a focus-centred and play-based yoga service promoting well-being and empowerment for children with autism, sensory processing challenges, anxiety and stress. Using strengths, interests, play, and yoga to experience and work towards high level well-being and living life fully is a significant part of services. Matty strives to make yoga and meditation easily accessible and aims to teach how to do the same by promoting sensory, energy, motor, and emotional wellness strategies.

OUR
IDEA

Matty is a specialized yoga and play product for children with autism, sensory processing challenges, anxiety, and stress. Providing focus-based yoga, meditation and interactive stories. Matty use purposeful play and yoga therapy techniques to make easily accessible for children with autism. We play, run, jump, and balance, breathe, engage in poses, explore our feelings, relax, and all the rest. To make it accessible to children with ASD, it must be adapted and it should be fun! Matty simply does the job.
